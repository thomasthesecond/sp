Surgery Partners is a leading operator of surgical facilities and ancillary services with more than 150 locations nationwide. We provide exceptional integrated healthcare experiences between our providers and patients.

What we do

Our diverse company operates multiple types of healthcare businesses dedicated to improving the quality of care in a convenient and cost-effective manner. The support of our ancillary services is one of many unique attributes that differentiates us from our competitors. These services include multi-specialty physician practices, urgent care facilities, anesthesia services, a diagnostic laboratory, specialty pharmacy services, and optical services.

How we’re different

Our integrated approach to advancing markets allows for flexibility to provide care on an individualized, local market basis. Whether entering a new market with surgical facilities, ancillary services or joint ventures with health systems, or furthering an existing market’s growth potential by focusing on base business, in-market development and new service lines, our experience has shown us that no two markets are alike. We see value in individuality.

Each community we serve is unique, so we work with our partners to develop a customized strategy for each facility. We are a leading healthcare services company with a differentiated outpatient delivery model focused on providing high quality, cost effective solutions for surgical and related ancillary care in support of our patients and physicians, as evidenced by our historically strong patient satisfaction rates, physician retention trends, and industry-leading track record of growth. The acquisition of NSH, creates a leading, national musculoskeletal platform in the United States.

How we got here

Far more than a business arrangement, Surgery Partners has a rich history of reaching out to its partners, maintaining accessibility to resources and expertise rarely found in our industry. Surgery Partners' story is as simple as its business style: We deliver quality at an exceptional value. We make healthcare local, and more importantly, personal.

2004

Our story began in 2004 with the formation of Surgery Partners with a physician-centric operating philosophy.

2011

We merged with NovaMed in 2011 with a focus on ophthalmology services.

2014

After achieving a milestone of ten successful years, we merged with Symbion Healthcare, a large multispecialty provider of ambulatory surgery centers and hospitals.

2015

On October 1, 2015, Surgery Partners completed an Initial Public Offering (NASDAQ: SGRY).

2017

In 2017 we announced the acquisition of National Surgical Healthcare, representing a unique opportunity to establish the largest standalone, independent surgical facilities operator in the United States, with a strong presence in musculoskeletal procedures.

Today

We are an industry leader in surgical services, with a differentiated outpatient delivery model backed by our suite of ancillary service offerings in support our physicians, including multi-specialty physician practices, urgent care facilities, anesthesia services, laboratory services, specialty pharmacy services, and optical services.
