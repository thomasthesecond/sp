        <div class="container">
            <div class="column">
                <h2>How we got here</h2>

                <p>
                    Far more than a business arrangement, Surgery Partners has a rich history of reaching out to its partners, maintaining accessibility to resources and expertise rarely found in our industry. Surgery Partners' story is as simple as its business style: We
                    deliver quality at an exceptional value. We make healthcare local, and more importantly, personal.
                </p>

                <div class="Timeline">
                    <h3 class="Timeline-headline">
                        2004
                    </h3>

                    <p class="Timeline-description">
                        Our story began in 2004 with the formation of Surgery Partners with a physician-centric operating philosophy.
                    </p>
                    <h3 class="Timeline-headline">
                        2011
                    </h3>

                    <p class="Timeline-description">
                        We merged with NovaMed in 2011 with a focus on ophthalmology services.
                    </p>
                    <h3 class="Timeline-headline">
                        2014
                    </h3>

                    <p class="Timeline-description">
                        After achieving a milestone of ten successful years, we merged with Symbion Healthcare, a large multispecialty provider of ambulatory surgery centers and hospitals.
                    </p>
                    <h3 class="Timeline-headline">
                        2015
                    </h3>

                    <p class="Timeline-description">
                        On October 1, 2015, Surgery Partners completed an Initial Public Offering (NASDAQ: SGRY).
                    </p>
                    <h3 class="Timeline-headline">
                        2017
                    </h3>

                    <p class="Timeline-description">
                        In 2017 we announced the acquisition of National Surgical Healthcare, representing a unique opportunity to establish the largest standalone, independent surgical facilities operator in the United States, with a strong presence in musculoskeletal procedures.
                    </p>
                    <h3 class="Timeline-headline">
                        Today
                    </h3>

                    <p class="Timeline-description">
                        We are an industry leader in surgical services, with a differentiated outpatient delivery model backed by our suite of ancillary service offerings in support our physicians, including multi-specialty physician practices, urgent care facilities, anesthesia
                        services, laboratory services, specialty pharmacy services, and optical services.
                    </p>
                </div>
